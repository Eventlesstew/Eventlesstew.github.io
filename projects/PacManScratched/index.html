EDITOR CHANGES

Reworked how lives are handled in the Editor.

  • When you start playing a maze, your lives are finite, but they will be set to infinite when opening up the editor, even when going back to playtesting.
  • This allows lives to keep their finite nature when playing other people's mazes, but when using the Editor, they will be kept infinite to prevent too many unfortunate mishaps from kicking you back to the menu with your progress being lost.
  • “But Event, can't they just open and close the editor to get infinite lives and cheese my maze?” They can also set it to infinite in the settings. >:)

THE MAZE EDITOR IS BACK!!!

The Maze Editor has returned as the Maze Workshop, found in the Maze setting.

Fun fact: There was a Maze editor in Pac-Man Scratched since the very beginning, which is how I managed to recreate all of the mazes you love. Now it has been polished for players to use as well.

I should also mention the differences from the old, separate, bad Maze Editor project too.

  • NO MORE LAG! - The Maze Editor has now been optimised to run much greater than 5 FPS.
  • Actually useful guides - Now there's a proper UI showing you what you can do.
  • A different way to export - Instead of remixing the project, you now just Press “T” and show off your maze to others!
  • Infinite Lives! - This should help when editing mazes by preventing Game Over's from ruining your work, or if you're trying to beat someone's near-impossible Maze. Remember, you can go back to the menu by clicking the Green Flag.

v2.0

MAJOR UPDATE! WE HIT THE TWO OH!

We got lots of Pac-Mania related content this update!

NEW ADDITIONS

  • PAC MANIA GAME STYLE - Sue and the Commons have joined the Ghost party! Now you'll have to deal with them when clearing mazes.
    • Sue and Funky will constantly chase the player.
    • Spunky will chase Pac-Man and scatter towards the top right corner in a similar manner to Blinky, except she doesn't become aggressive when there are a few dots left though.

MENU REDESIGN

  • The menu has been fully reworked! Now all of the settings are on the left in one massive menu with the Maze preview and a description of the selected setting on the right.
  • Unfortunately, I removed the mouse controls and reduced the Maze preview to just the Maze itself. They were a bit much of a hassle to keep.

NEW SETTINGS

  • MUSIC - You can now listen to your favourite tunes from Pac-Mania or Pac & Pal during gameplay. You can even let the maze automatically decide what mania music should be played through the “Auto” option. New settings now include Music and Ability.
  • ABILITY - You can now make Pac-Man jump or speed up by pressing the Spacebar.
  • PRESET - This will automatically set everything up for you if you just want to go in and play the classics.

SETTING CHANGES

  • Bonus Life Threshold has been renamed to Extend Threshold and now has the option to get a bonus life every 5K, 50K, 100K or 150K points.
  • Elroy Indicator has been reworked as Aggressive Blinky for easier understanding. There is now an option to disable this mode entirely.
  • Pinky/Inky offset bug has been renamed to just “Offset Bug” to include any future ghosts that may use an offset target tile.

GAMEPLAY CHANGES

  • Frightened ghosts have slowed down. This should hopefully allow you to rack up a large chomp streak easier.
  • If Pac-Man hasn't eaten a dot for 4 seconds, a ghost will exit the ghost house if there are any still in there.
  • Added music for every maze.

INTERNAL CHANGES

  • Separated the X and Y values to allow jumping.
  • The Fruit is now built into the Entities Sprite and the Scatter/Chase algorithm is also built into the Tile Sprite. This means that the game now has two fewer sprites.

And of course, there are bugfixes.
