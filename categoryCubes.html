ALL CUBES IN BLOCKBUSTERS

BLU

Blu's all rounded nature makes him a versatile combatant in any situation thrown at him.
That plus his basic as heck abilities makes him a suitable choice for those completely new
to Blockbusters.

SPECIAL: Super Dash

Blu performs a powerful dash that boasts a quick recharge time and extra reach.

SECONDARY: Super Spin

Blu performs an enhanced spin that deals extra damage.

QUIRK: All Rounded

Uhhhh... yeah Blu straight up has no quirk. No strengths means no weaknesses.

CRIM

Blu's best friend and biggest rival, Crim's tough as a rock.

SPECIAL: Power Dash

Crim performs a powerful dash that deals a good chunk of damage.

SECONDARY: Rock Shield

Crim creates a shield that protects him from damage until it breaks

QUIRK: Heavyweight

Crim is a hard hitter and can soak up hits like a tank. He does suffer from slower movement and ability recharges though.
